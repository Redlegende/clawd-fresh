## Dr. William Davis's Stance

Dr. Davis **explicitly recommends legumes** as prebiotic fiber sources:

> "Legumes ‚Äî kidney beans, black beans, white beans, chickpeas, other starchy beans, and lentils can be rich sources of the galactooligosaccharide, or GOS, form of prebiotic fibers. Hummus (pureed chickpeas) is another convenient source."

### Prebiotic Fiber Content (per 1/2 cup cooked):

| Food | Prebiotic Fiber | Net Carbs |
|------|-----------------|-----------|
| **Chickpeas / Hummus** | 8 grams | 13.5g |
| **Lentils** | 2.5 grams | 11g |
| **White beans** | ~7.6 grams | 12g |

### Dr. Davis's Recommendations:
- **Portion limit:** 1/4 to 1/2 cup cooked per serving
- **Max net carbs:** 15g per serving
- **Goal:** Include as part of your 20g daily prebiotic fiber target
- **Caution:** Legumes contain amylopectin C (less digestible than grain amylopectin A, but still impacts blood sugar)

## ‚ö†Ô∏è SIBO/IBS Considerations

Legumes are **high FODMAP** foods due to galacto-oligosaccharides (GOS) ‚Äî the same compound that makes them prebiotic can cause bloating/gas in SIBO sufferers.

### The Science:
- Oligosaccharides are **water-soluble**
- They leach out of legumes into cooking liquid
- Canned legumes are lower FODMAP than dried
- Proper preparation significantly reduces FODMAP content

---

## üõ†Ô∏è Preparation Methods for SIBO-Friendly Legumes

### Method 1: Canned (Easiest)
‚úÖ **Best option for SIBO/IBS**
- FODMAPs have already leached into the can liquid
- Rinse thoroughly before eating
- **Safe portion:** 1/4 cup chickpeas or 1/2 cup lentils

### Method 2: Overnight Soak + Strain (Dried Legumes)
1. Soak dried lentils/beans overnight in water
2. Discard soaking water (contains leached FODMAPs)
3. Cook in fresh water
4. **Strain after cooking** ‚Äî don't consume the cooking liquid
5. Monash University research confirms this reduces FODMAP content

### Method 3: Pressure Cooking
- Pressure cooking for 60+ minutes on high
- Let cool naturally in pot for another hour
- Results in well-cooked, more digestible legumes
- Community reports better tolerance with this method

### Method 4: Extended Boiling (Monash-Tested)
- Boil red lentils or kidney beans for 30 minutes
- FODMAP reduction occurs progressively every 5 minutes up to 30 minutes
- Strain and discard cooking liquid
